\begin{frame}
	\frametitle{Conclusion}
	\begin{itemize}
		\item Goal: use early-stage design optimization to find the most viable WEC design
		\item Contribution:
		\item Techno-economic and environmental metrics reflecting net value to system
		\item Framework to incorporate capacity expansion model in design optimization
		\item Implemented LCOE minimization, achieved 57% cost reduction
		\item Thorough plan to improve technical modules and extend design space
		\item Limitations
		\item Metrics currently do not encompass social impact and certain PBE markets
		\item Price-taker assumption inaccurate for large WEC penetration
	\end{itemize}
\end{frame}
